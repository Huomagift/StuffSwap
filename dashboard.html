What is Stuff Swap

Stuff Swap is a platform that connects people who want to swap items. Instead of throwing things away, trade them with someone just blocks away.

Stuff Swap connects you with nearby users by ZIP code who are looking to swap gently-used items - clothes, books, toys, and more.
